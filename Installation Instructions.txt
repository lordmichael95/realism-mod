Installation Instructions:

1. Click on the download link
2. You will be taken to a MediaFire link, click on the green load button on the bottom right-hand side
3. Once the file has finished downloading, extract the file to: user/Libraies/My Documents/Paradox Interactive/Crusader Kings II/mod (if you haven't used mods before, you may have to make the mod folder)
4. Run the game, and in the startup window, check mark the checkbox that is this mod
5. Play the game and enjoy
